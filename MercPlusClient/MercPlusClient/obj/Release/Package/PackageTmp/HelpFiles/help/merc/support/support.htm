Support for MERC+  


In case Vendors/shops experience problems they are unable to solve, either themselves or via Website for shops, they must call the local MSL agent responsible for Equipment Maintenance and Repair.
If you as the local Maersk agent are unable or unsure of the answer, your next step would be to contact the appropriate area Super User from the list below, who will be able to assist you. This also applies if you, as local Maersk agent, experience problems using MERC+.
In case where super users are not able to help, they will take contact to system administrator in order to solve the problem. We kindly ask all of you to respect the "chain" of communication as it will enable all of us to learn from the problems we are experiencing.
