GETTING PAID AUTOMATICALLY

When approval has been received on an estimate, and the repair work has been done, the estimate is converted into a work order. This work order can also serve as a "paperless" invoice and if so, is transmitted automatically to the Maersk Line mainframe accounting system (RRIS) for payment.

Vendors will have access (via internet) to status of their work orders. Vendors will soon know - once work orders has been approved, payment is automatic.

RRIS, the Maersk accounting system, will pay all Maersk STS & Non-STS work orders.

Non-MERC+ payments will NOT be included with MERC+ payments - this to avoid confusion when vendor is posting payments

Methods of payment -  Paper check or Wire Transfer

Notifications - With each payment, MERC+ will send electronic file of reference numbers & amounts.
