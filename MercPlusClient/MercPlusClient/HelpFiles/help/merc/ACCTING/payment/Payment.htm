Payment
Basically the payment of vendors will be done in one of two below mentioned ways.
As per default the vendors are being paid manually, but if the local laws allow automatic payment, MERC+ will be able to accommodate and can be set up accordingly.
MERC+ is also able to handle the calculation of vat and sales tax if applicable in your country.
