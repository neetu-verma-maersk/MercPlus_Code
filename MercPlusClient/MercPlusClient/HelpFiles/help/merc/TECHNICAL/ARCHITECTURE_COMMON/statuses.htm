MERC+ Work Order State Definitions:

How statuses related to one another (diagram)

 

Status Code

Description

Purpose

000

Draft – saved, but not submitted to MSL.

Newly created ‘draft’ copy of a work order. Visible only to Shop.

100

Rejected

Work order not accepted. – Can be deleted or resubmitted – visible to shop.

130

Rejected for local MSL review

MSL must review before changing and/or rejecting W\O (100)

140

Rejected for review by CPH

Work order must be reviewed by CPH before setting to final 100 rejected status

200

Pending MSL Approval

MSL to reject (100), approve(390) or forward to CPH review(330). Authorized MSL can forward to Central Equipment as well(340).

310

Suspended for review by MSL.

MSL can approve (390), reject (100), or forward to CPH (330) and/or Central Equipment for review (340)

320

Suspended for review by MSL for final forwarding to CPH

MSL must review work order and mark as ‘seen’ and then forward  to CPH for suspension review (330)

330

Suspended for review by CPH

CPH can reject (130) for local MSL review or approve (390) or forward to Central Equipment for suspension review (340)

340

Suspended for review by Central Equipment

Central equipment can approve (390) or reject for CPH review(140) or reject for local MSL review (130)

390

Approved Estimate

Estimate approved and waiting for completion by shop. Any alterations will force re-validation and possible status changes. (Process repeated)

400

Completed work order

Completed work order status.

500

RRIS transmitted

Work Order invoice sent to RRIS

550

RRIS rejected

Work Order rejected by RRIS

600

RRIS accepted

Work Order accepted by RRIS

800

Paid

Currently not in use by MERC

900

Processed

Transmitted to RKRP if required

9999

Deleted Estimate/Work Order

Logical delete – not accessible.
