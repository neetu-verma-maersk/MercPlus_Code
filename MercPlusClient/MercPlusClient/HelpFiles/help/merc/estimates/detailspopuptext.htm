VIEW DETAILS

  • Vendor Ref (optional) – This field has space for 10 characters which can be filled in by the vendor, or it can be left blank. If the field is left blank, MERC+ will generate one on its own. The number will be used as reference in future payments.
  • Estimate Number - the sequence number given by the system on the estimate
  • Exchange Rate - the exchange rate from the local currency to 100 US Dollar
  • Preparation time - the total preparation time for current estimate

  • ORDINARY HOURS - the number of man hours spending in the normal working days.

  • OT1/OT2/OT3 - the number of man hours spending in the non-working days.
  • Hours - the number of the man hours spent in respective item.
  • Local Cost - total expense spend under according item.
  • Total Labour Cost excluding Tax - the sum of all labour cost excluding Taxes.
  • Cost of numbered parts supplied by shop - the total expense of spare parts updated under numbered parts in the estimate.
  • Cost of other materials supplied by shop - the total expense of spare parts updated under material cost in the estimate.
  • Total shop supplied material cost excluding tax - The sum of all repair shop supplied materials, excluding taxes.
  • Total owner supplied parts excluding tax - The total cost of parts/materials supplied by Maersk Line.
  • Tax Rates - If tax applicable according to local law, MERC+ will be updated with details and you will be able to see detailed tax calculation here. If you wish to have more information, please contact local Maersk Line agency.
