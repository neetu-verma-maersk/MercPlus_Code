General information about MERC+
 
MERC+ is a new Maersk repair and maintenance system which allows repair shops input, edit, submit and store repair information and get estimate approvals from local Maersk Line office through the system. It also enables the daily query and tracing of the status of the repair estimates as well as the payment through the system.
