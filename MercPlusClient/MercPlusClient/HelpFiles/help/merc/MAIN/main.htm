Problem Reporting Procedure (Including Problems with MERC+)

1)
Any EMR related matter, technical, operational, system, where an agent
have a problem/question must be reported directly to his/her Area EMR
manager. CEN should not be copied unless it is felt necessary.

Area EMR manager will then look into issue and if GLBEMR involvement is
needed the Area EMR manager will go to GLBEMR.

Whether necessary or not to copy is decided by the individual agent but
it is obviously not necessary to cc GLBEMR when an estimate needs to be
approved (as we very often see).

No matter what issue, your Area EMR manager must always be in copy when
you write to GLBEMR otherwise they may loose valuable information.

Area EMR has taken over many of the old CEN functions and CEN are no
longer manned to advise directly to agents hence pls. respect to go via
your Area EMR managers.

2)
In case of system problems outside CPH office hours Area EMR managers
must go directly to Global Helpdesk themselves.
