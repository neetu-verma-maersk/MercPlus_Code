End of Month - problems in pulling MERC+ reports
Usually pulling a report is comparatively fast with the report arriving within 5 to 10 seconds.
At month end many users are pulling billing reports and this will occasionally delay the reports, sometimes up to several minutes.

As long as you see a PC icon in lower left corner on your screen with an arrow pointing towards the PC icon saying "Downloading from site….." or "Done" something is happening and you should NOT disturb it, instead have a cup of coffee or do something else. Only if you receive "The page cannot be displayed" error in your browser can you close the page.

IF you close the page while report is being pulled, the report pulling criteria's are stored in your browser. Next time you want pull a report these stored criteria are used to pull the report, even if you enter something else on MERC+ report request screen, i.e. you will pull the same report time after time. This is not the fault of MERC+ but rather part of Microsoft IE Explorer design. If you are experiencing this problem the only solution is to restart your computer in order to clear all temporary folders.

Pls. remember to inform your repair shops accordingly.
