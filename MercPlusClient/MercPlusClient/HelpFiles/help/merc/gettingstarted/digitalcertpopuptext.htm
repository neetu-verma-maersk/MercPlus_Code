REGISTRATION FORM

  • Choose your area – Please select from drop down menu the area where you are located.
  • City code or location - Please enter the city code or location where you are based e.g. CPH 
  • Country/territory - Please select your country/territory from drop down menu e.g. Denmark
  • Location/city code - Here you can state the name of location/city where you want access to al the shops.
  • Your business e-mail - Please enter your business e-mail e.g. GLBEMRops@maersk.com
  • Repair shop number(s) - Here you should fill in the repair shop number(s) you need access for.
  • Your @maersk id - Please fill in your unique @maersk id
  • Country/territory - select the country where you located
  • Your business Phone - Your phone number , fill in the format of 'Country - area - number'.
  • Your business Email - your company email address
  • Comments - notes given in addition to the information give in the above column.
  • OK - the registration form will be submitted after press the button
  • Clear fields - all the information filled will be cleared after click the button

DIGITAL CERTIFICATE

  • Renew digital certificate - the Digital Certificate is valid for one year. System Administrator will send you an email notification one month before your certificate expires asking if you want to renew the certificate. This button leads you to the same registration form applying the digital certificate before. This is the only correct way to renew the certificate.

System Administrator will send you an e-mail of granting the availability of the renewed digital certificate with the SAME ID you had before. You are asked to download it from 'Download digital certificate' for installation.
