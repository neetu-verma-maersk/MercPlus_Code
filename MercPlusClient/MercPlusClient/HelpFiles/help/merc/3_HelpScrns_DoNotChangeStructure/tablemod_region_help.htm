MERC+ user management
 
Region
  • Regions are groups of locations which are combined to form a region
  • Regions are primarily for persons responsible for multiple locations - having these locations defined as a region allows that person to pull reports by region.
  • A region is flexible - it can be:
    • A group of locations within a country
      • example - Southeast US
    • All locations in more than one country
      • example - all locations in Netherlands, Belgium & Luxemburg could form region called Benelux

TIPS

  • WARNING - As regions are defined within MERC+, care must be taken to insure locations do not overlap (ie 1 location associated with 2 regions) and that any new locations within a region are added to that region.
  • The use of regions is optional - regions are not required
