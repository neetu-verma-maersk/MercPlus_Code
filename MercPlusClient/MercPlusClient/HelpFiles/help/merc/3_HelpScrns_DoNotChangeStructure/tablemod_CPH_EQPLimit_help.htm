MERC+ CPH Approval Level management
 
COPENHAGEN LEVEL
  • The CPH level is the way that GLBEMR and CENEQUSAL search out units which are too expensive to repair for the age of the unit.
  • The CPH level is set by CPH
  • The CPH level is based on:
    • Age of the unit
    • Type of unit
    • Material (steel or aluminum)
    • The COUNTRY FACTOR - which is a percentage assigned to each country for each equipment type. The COUNTRY FACTOR is used to modify the CPH level to account for repair costs, demand etc.

TIP

  • Estimates which exceed the CPH factor are received at local MSL as STATUS LEVEL of   320 SUSPENDED FOR REVIEW BY CPH.
    • Local MSL can either
      • adds their comments to the estimate (general condition etc.) and presses the SUBMIT TO CPH button.
  • This means means local MSL can either:
    • Reject the estimate or
    • Pass to CPH for disposition after adding comments which would assist CPH in its decision.
