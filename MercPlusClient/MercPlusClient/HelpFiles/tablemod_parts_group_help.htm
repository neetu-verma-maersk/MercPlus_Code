PARTS GROUPS

 

GLBEMR CEN87011 JAS  04.01.21
Master Parts - Part Group
Part Group codes denotes to which reefer unit type the part belong.


RDST eg means Reefer Daikin Standard

RCCA means Reefer Carrier Controlled Atmosphere

RCCH means Reefer Carrier Controlled atmosphere with Humidity control.

RCCO means Reefer Carrier Common meaning the part is common for several units
 

UNAS - UNASSIGNED GROUP CODES and use this for all Master Parts that do not have a group code. This will allow you to query the Unassigned group, and subsequently update those needed to other groups.
